Road Construction

Road construction

Sustainable road construction practices focus on reducing energy consumption, conserving natural resources, minimising pollution, and improving the overall longevity and resilience of roads.

  • We use energy efficient equipment to reduce the fuel consumption.
  • We use recycled concrete as base or sub-base material for the roads to reduce the use of new aggregates.
  • We implement strategies to reduce, reuse, and recycle construction waste materials.
  • We design roads with wildlife crossings like bridges and tunnels to allow animals to safely cross, reducing the risk of collisions.
  • We implement noise barriers and other measures to minimise the impact of road traffic noise on nearby communities.
